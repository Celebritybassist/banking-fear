Update for New York Residents

For New York residents faced with overwhelming bills, assistance is now available. In the last month alone, more than 9,400 residents reached out for help to learn about the options available to individuals and families in need of lower monthly payments.

Programs to help state residents are designed to help those facing a genuine personal, family, or business difficulty. This often leads to financial stress that forces consumers to rely on credit accounts to take care of day-to-day expenses.

Most often, it is the unexpected life event that leads to financial difficulty, such as a reduction in income, medical expenses, or even a business downturn experienced by business owners and sole proprietors.

With inflation continuing to put pressure on finances in 2022, It is expected that throughout the year, more and more consumers will be reaching out for assistance and taking advantage of programs specifically designed to assist those struggling with unmanageable bills.
